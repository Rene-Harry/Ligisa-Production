ABOUT US
Background

Ligisa Youth Network is a community based organization founded in the year 2021 to provide a platform for discussing factors affecting youth in Homabay County and implementing projects on youth empowerment. Three focus areas covered by the organization are health, education and personal finance.

Mission

To provide a platform through which the youth can work collaboratively to reach their full potential in the fields of health, education and personal finance.

Vision

To provide an inspiring community hub which runs programs to empower the youth of Homabay County on health, education and personal finance.
