Ligisa Empowered To Excell

Who We Are

Ligisa Youth Network is a community based organization founded in the year 2021 to provide a platform for discussing factors affecting youth in Homabay County and implementing projects on youth empowerment. Three focus areas covered by the organization are health, education and personal finance.

Health

Substance abuse, sexually transmitted infections and early pregnancy are health challenges affecting many youths in Homabay County. They not only cause immediate lowering of quality of life but also set the youth on a downward spiraling path. By increasing awareness of these conditions in the community and facilitating access to information on available support the organization aims to mitigate the impact of these conditions. The objectives of the organization as regards youth health is:

Create awareness on drug and substance abuse

Create awareness on sexually transmitted infections

Create awareness on early pregnancy in Homabay County and its impact at a personal and community level.

Education

Homabay County has a primary school enrollment of 98%. This number reduces to about 58% in secondary school and dwindles even further by the time youths get to tertiary level. Reasons for dropping out include teenage pregnancies, lack of school fees and lack of role models. Ligisa will support youth to pursue education both formally and informally to levels they desire in the following ways:

  • Running computer literacy programs- through this means the world will be opened up to the youth and they will get motivation from what their peers are doing around the world. Additionally, it will increase their access to scholarship programs which they would source online.
  • Promoting access to library services- this will provide a safe space for studying. It will also enable access to a wide range of books which will broaden the perspective of the youth.
  • Computer based courses- these are particularly of advantage to youths who have limited access to finance for their studies. They also help the youth acquire more skills which boosts their employability.
  • Life skills training- Life skills training is recommended by WHO in helping to reduce early pregnancy. They are helpful in assisting in the transition from school life to work life.
  • Career mentorship programs- through this program the youth will be in a position to make informed choices on their career path. This will expose them to various careers which are available and help in choosing ones that they are passionate about.

Personal Finance

The youth in Homabay County are greatly affected by poverty. This has far reaching consequences like promoting early sexual debut, dropping out of school and drug and substance abuse. There are limited employment opportunities in the county. Through personal finance classes Ligisa hopes to help change the mindset of the youth to create opportunities for self employment. Secondly through investment classes they can sustain financial stability in the long run. The organization will also educate the youth on how to start and run businesses as well as how to source for seed capital. Programs to be run under this department include;

  • Personal finance classes
  • Investment classes
  • Debt management
  • Basic bookkeeping
