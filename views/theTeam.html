Meet The Team

Ligisa Youth network is run by a team of fantastic staff and volunteers

Javan Malowa Secretary

Javan Otieno Malowa is a Diploma graduate in Applied Horticulture from Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology, set to graduate in December 2024. With practical experience gained from an internship at VEGPRO Gorge company in Naivasha, Javan has honed skills in grafting avocado varieties, scouting crops such as pak choi and broccoli, and exploring pack house operations.

Rene Harrizon Treasurer

Rene is passionate about technology and empowering youth with essential digital skills. Committed to bridging the digital divide, Rene helps young people thrive in today’s tech-driven world through education and innovation.

Felix Kidera Treasurer

Felix is highly organized and results-driven professional with strong expertise in management. He is adept at balancing multiple priorities while maintaining a focus on delivering high-quality outcomes. He possess strong problem-solving skills, effective communication abilities, and a leadership style that fosters collaboration and team development.

Ivy Zenani Treasurer

A passionate advocate for food and nutrition security, she is motivated by the belief in the fundamental right to food and is deeply inspired by the Food Justice Movement. She works to promote agrifood systems resilience and reduce inequities in local communities. Her vision is to bridge the gaps between food security, diet quality, andequity.
